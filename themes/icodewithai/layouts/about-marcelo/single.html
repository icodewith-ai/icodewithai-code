About Marcelo

I code and build apps with AI

I code and build apps using modern development tools powered by AI—with a focus on MVPs, proof-of-concepts, utilities, and experiments. I love diving into early ideas, rapidly iterating, and using those prototypes to test and validate concepts before moving into full-on planning or production.

With over 30 years in the tech industry, I’ve worn many hats—developer, project manager, podcaster, editor, designer, VP, and founder. I’ve worked with companies like Walt Disney Imagineering, NBC, Toyota, and J.F. Shea Inc., gaining hands-on experience across industries and disciplines.

I’ve also founded and scaled five startups—all successfully acquired. I bring a hands-on, forward-thinking mindset to every project, blending strategy and execution to get things done.
