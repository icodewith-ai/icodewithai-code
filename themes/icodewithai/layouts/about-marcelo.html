About Marcelo

I'm the founder of iCodeWith.ai. I'm a Vibe Coder Advocate. I build apps with AI. I love learning and sharing.

Software creation is changing fast. AI now lets anyone with a technical mindset build automations, tools and even full apps simply by talking to a language model. This shift has given rise to vibe coders: product managers, marketers, analysts and other problem-solvers who understand their business deeply but don't necessarily write code. They're shipping solutions every day, yet they often lack standards, guidance or community.

I created iCodeWith.ai to help support this new generation of builders. My mission is to teach people how to vibe-code by structuring their conversations with AI so they produce maintainable, production-ready apps. I also created Vibedocs, a free framework that adds structure to creative AI bursts, and use it to build prototypes ranging from MVPs to internal tools and experiments to show what’s possible. I also made it available via GitHub to allow others to use it in their projects.

As a Vibe Coder Advocate, I bridge AI tools and real-world business problems. I'm fluent in leading AI coding platforms, including Claude Code, Cursor, Amazon Kiro and Gemini Code Assist, and use them collectively to create solutions. I build prompt libraries, use-case playbooks, guided workflows and deliver live demos and interactive content. I also host the “Coding with AI” podcast, run a monthly meetup in Orange County, write tutorials and blog articles and continuously enhance the iCodeWith.ai site using the vibe‑coding techniques I teach.

I love to foster community by creating safe, supportive spaces both online and in person where vibe coders can share, learn and grow.
